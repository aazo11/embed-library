Our Story

HiGeorge was born from our own experiences and observations as readers. We wanted all news publishers, not only the largest ones, to be able to impactfully use data journalism and visualizations in their reporting.

So, at HiGeorge, we create engaging, interactive, and contextual data visualizations to help newsrooms better leverage data for their readers. We make sense of data so publishers don’t have to.

By supporting and strengthening publishers across the country, we aim to help ensure that all readers can be well-informed about their own communities.
