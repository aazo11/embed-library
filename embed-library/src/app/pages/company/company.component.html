Our Mission

We create engaging, contextual, and interactive data visualizations that enhance storytelling while creating a new revenue stream.

We specifically designed HiGeorge for newsrooms that want an easy way to supplement a wide array of articles with rich data interactives. HiGeorge maximizes time spent on site and translates this increased engagement into monetization opportunities for publishers by allowing readers to take actions directly from the visualization without leaving the site.

HiGeorge is 100% free for publishers, includes the necessary datasets, and requires no coding whatsoever.
