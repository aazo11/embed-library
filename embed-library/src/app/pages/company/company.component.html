Our Mission

Separating fact from fiction with a dose of data

We create engaging, interactive, and contextual data visualizations that enhance storytelling and further reader understanding.

We specifically designed HiGeorge so it would be easy for journalists to supplement their articles with rich data interactives. With HiGeorge, publishers can overcome the burden of manual data entry and completely bypass the need for any data background or experience. Journalists focus on the narrative, and we supply the data.

All of our visualizations can be localized and customized to meet each publisher’s requirements, so HiGeorge can be used by any newsroom in America. By supporting and strengthening publishers across the country, we aim to ensure that all readers can be well-informed about their own communities.
